Discuss, Learn, Hack

Format of CosmoLab is fluid, and we adjust it to our needs each semester. In general, we combine short presentations on most recent research progress and news, longer presentations on classical papers and important concepts in astropysics, and hacks on astrophysical coding tools that are of interest to this community. We all rotate in contributing to presentations and discussions. Our goal is to continue learning and building the tools we need for research, in a collaborative setting.

If you are a new graduate or undergraduate student, curious about research in cosmology, or just wish to check out the group and get in touch, please send a message below!

Who we are

Grads, undergrads, postdocs, staff, and faculty studying cosmology and astrophysics at USC. We come together weekly to catch up and hack. This is a safe space for everyone with a passion for this field to learn, ask questons, and build our local community.
